How it works

1
Target a LinkedIn event
Point the workflow at any LinkedIn event URL. Make watches and triggers instantly when new attendees appear.
2
Apify scrapes attendee data
Two Apify actors run in sequence — one to pull the event list, one to extract full profile data.
3
Data lands in Google Sheets
Each lead is added as a new row with name, title, company and contact details — clean and ready.
4
You get an email alert
Gmail sends you an instant notification about the event lead — so you can jump in, make the call, and close the client before anyone else does.

No chasing dashboards. No missed leads. Just an email that tells you exactly who to call and when.
